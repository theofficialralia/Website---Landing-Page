FAQ

Questions, answered.

General

What is Ralia?

Ralia is a digital platform that connects organizations and brands looking for growth with promoters who share campaigns to drive visibility and engagement.

Is Ralia just for high-level social media influencers?

No. While we work with social media creators, Ralia focuses on real-world reach. Our network consists of real people who build genuine trust through direct digital sharing, physical community campaigns, and event participation.

Who can create a campaign on Ralia?

Anyone looking to spread the word about a project can use Ralia. This includes corporate businesses, tech startups, event organizers hosting conferences, and religious organizations or pastors promoting church programs.

How do I contact Ralia?

For general inquiries, partnerships, or questions, email us at hello@ralia.co. For account issues or technical assistance, reach out to support@ralia.co.

For Promoters

How do I earn on Ralia?

You earn financial rewards by selecting available campaigns, sharing them with your audience, or participating in specific campaign activities.

Do I need thousands of social media followers to join as a promoter?

No. We value genuine influence and real-world trust over follower counts. If you can mobilize your local community, friends, colleagues, or physical network, you're welcome to promote on Ralia.

When and how do I get paid?

Payouts are processed directly to your linked bank account every Friday, once campaign conditions are successfully met and verified.

Are there any fees to join as a promoter?

No. Joining Ralia and browsing available campaigns is completely free for all promoters.

For Campaign Creators

What types of campaigns can I run on Ralia?

We offer highly efficient, trust-driven campaigns — including targeted digital promotion, experiential event participation, and localized door-to-door or community campaigns that reach real, verified users.

How do I launch a campaign?

Create an organizer profile, fund your campaign wallet, set your promotional requirements — budget, targets, and assets — and launch it to our network.

Is there a minimum budget required?

Ralia is built to be scalable. You can set a budget that fits your goals, whether it's a large-scale product launch or a localized event promotion.

How does Ralia ensure campaign quality?

We use internal verification metrics to track promoter engagement and ensure your materials are distributed authentically, according to your campaign rules.

Ready to grow?

Start a campaign or start promoting — the network is ready when you are.
